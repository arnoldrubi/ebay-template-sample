About Award Products

Texas is the truck capital of the USA and like our neighbors we love trucks. We began in 2005 selling truck accessories locally to our Texan family. Award Products is a family owned and operated re-seller located in Katy, Texas.

Over the years we have gained product knowledge in Jeeps, ATV, Motorcycles, and of course tires. We have also built close and trusted relationships with distributors and manufacturers. We have one of the most seasoned and knowledgeable customer service and support networks for our customers.

Now, 10 years later we are expanding across neighboring states and selling across the country. We invite you to shop with us and join our family.
